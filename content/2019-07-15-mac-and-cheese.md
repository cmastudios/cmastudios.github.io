Servings: 3

Ingredients
===
- 8 oz elbow macaroni
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- 1/4 tsp salt
- pepper
- 1 cup milk
- 4 oz shredded cheddar cheese
- paprika

Instructions
===
1. Cook macaroni
2. In separate pan, melt butter
3. Mix flour, salt, pepper, and milk
4. Boil and then cook 2 minutes until thicc
5. Mix in cheese
6. Drain macaroni
7. Mix macaroni and cheese, add paprika
